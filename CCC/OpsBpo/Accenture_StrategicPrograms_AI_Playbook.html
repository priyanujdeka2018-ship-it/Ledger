Case Study Framework — Use This Structure

Problem: What decision are senior leaders struggling with? (e.g., "Where are we leaking margin?")

User: Who is the executive user? What does their day look like? What information do they lack?

Data: What enterprise data feeds are needed? Finance, HR, Sales, Delivery — what's available vs. what needs integration?

AI Architecture: How do you design the agent workflow? Agent 1 ingests data → Agent 2 detects patterns → Agent 3 generates recommendations → Validation layer checks for accuracy and bias → Executive dashboard surfaces output.

Governance: How do you ensure executives trust the output? Explainability, audit trails, human-in-the-loop review for critical decisions.

Metrics: How do you measure success? Adoption rate, decision cycle time reduction, margin improvement attributable to the product, NPS from executive users.

Trade-offs: Build vs. buy? Speed vs. accuracy? Automation vs. human judgment? Show you think in trade-offs, not certainties.
