What this role actually is

Strip the pharma vocabulary and this is a transformation-program leadership seat inside Amgen's India capability centre, sitting between a global strategy org (OTDS) and the operations/technology teams that execute.

Stand up and grow the India-based Operations Transformation + Business Performance team. Establish priorities, execution discipline, and scalable ways of working. This is the "stand up a function and the operating model it runs on" job — your single most-repeated pattern.

Translate enterprise priorities into programs that improve efficiency, agility, performance visibility, and value realization. Output = measurable operational impact, not slideware.

Advance AI / analytics / automation and data-centric capability. Support delivery of Generative-AI use cases on enterprise data platforms using modern information-architecture and FAIR data principles. Note the framing: enable and orchestrate, not hand-code models.

Keep initiatives aligned, sequenced, governed, executed — with adoption outcomes tracked. This is RAID + governance cadence + value tracking at portfolio level.

5 · Cross-functional collaboration

Partner across OTDS (global), Operations leaders, DTI (Digital Technology & Innovation), and enterprise stakeholders. Integrated solutions across people · process · data · technology.

6 · Performance & change leadership

Build metrics, dashboards, governance mechanisms, value-tracking — while driving change management, capability building, and CI 2.0 adoption.

05 / Risk & Red Flags conditional · fired

What to verify before you commit

No confirmed distress signals — but two triggers fired and warrant questions on the recruiter call. Treat these as diligence, not deterrents.

"Build & Lead the AIN Capability" — new/scaling team risk

verify

A "build the capability" mandate is both your strength (greenfield = your pattern) and a risk (under-defined scope, thin headcount, unclear charter, reorg exposure). Ask: Is this team net-new or scaling an existing one? What's the current headcount & planned ramp? Who held this before me, and why is it open? Is the charter board-backed with multi-year funding?

Pharma regulatory operating context

context, not distress

Biotech is heavily regulated (GxP, validated systems, data integrity). This isn't a red flag — it's the operating reality. Ask: How regulated is this specific scope (GxP-adjacent vs commercial ops)? What's the validation overhead on AI/data deployments? It signals you understand the environment.

GCC maturity & cost-action exposure

verify

Global biotechs periodically run cost/restructuring actions that can touch capability centres. Ask: How long has AIN-Hyderabad operated? Is it in a growth or consolidation phase? Where does this function sit in the org's stated priorities? (A scan of recent Amgen India hiring volume is a quick sanity check.)

Level & comp ambiguity

verify

"Senior Manager" at Amgen India could be an M-band people-leader or shade toward Sr SM / Associate Director — a ₹15–20L swing. Ask early: internal level mapping, band, and whether the team-lead scope is confirmed or "to be built."

Net read

All four are verify-on-call items, none are walk-away signals. A greenfield transformation seat in your target city, at a marquee biotech, with your exact skill spine — the upside materially outweighs the diligence load.

Define how each initiative's business value is forecast, tracked, and confirmed — so impact isn't anecdotal.

Get the re-sequenced portfolio + value framework formally endorsed by the OTDS sponsor.

Scale ways of working across the team

Roll the operating model out fully; move from leader-dependent to self-running (your S12 pattern).
