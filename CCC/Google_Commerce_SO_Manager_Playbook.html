Reframe #1: Dues Collections = Payment Operations & Revenue Realisation

Your AED 12.97B annual collections programme IS payment operations at scale. Your 10-bank escrow infrastructure processing AED 442M daily IS multi-PSP partner management. Your delinquency governance IS payment conversion rate optimisation. The vocabulary is different; the capability is identical.

Reframe #2: T−25/T+30 Framework = Partnership Performance Intervention Model

When a payments partnership underperforms in a market, you need an intervention framework — when to escalate, what data to pull, which levers to push. Your T−25/T+30 critical collection window is exactly this: a data-driven intervention framework with defined trigger points and escalation paths. In Google language: "I designed a 55-day performance intervention framework that increased recovery rates and was adopted organisation-wide."

Google Pay works through PSPs (Payment Service Providers) — banks that provide the UPI rails. This maps directly to managing multiple payment partners with different integration specs, SLA structures, and performance metrics.
